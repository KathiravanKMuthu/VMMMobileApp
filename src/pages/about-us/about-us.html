About Us

Who We Are

Village Missionary Movement was started in 1996 by Brother K. David Ganesan. He could see Jesus and heard him cry and got the burden for the ministry and began to work. Many who have the same burden join together as a group beyond church denomination and work as a missionary movement.

Our Vision

As the verse, “Shepherd my Sheep” says children and youth in villages are reached and guided towards Jesus and through them gospel is taken to their villages.

To mold the child and youth as the future leader and mission workers

To make each Christian a soul winner
